FAQ

Questions We
Always Get.

I'm not a tech person at all. Can I actually use this? +
Yes — and that's exactly who we build this for. You don't use any software or log into any dashboard unless you want to. We build the system, we manage the system, and we fix it when something needs adjusting. Your only job is running your business. If you can send a text, you have all the technical ability this requires.
How long does it take to get set up? +
After your strategy call, we typically have your system built and live within about a week. It depends on the complexity of what we're building for your business, but we don't drag it out. We know you're losing leads while you're waiting, so we move fast.
Is this going to replace my office person or my crew? +
No. This handles the repetitive stuff — sending a first response, following up, organizing your leads — not the relationship-building or the actual work. If you have an office person, this makes them more effective by handling the tasks that eat their time. If it's just you, it makes you feel like you have a support system behind you.
How much does it cost? +
We build a custom proposal after your free call, based on your business size and what you need. We don't have a one-size-fits-all price because a solo operator and a company with 10 crews need very different systems. We'll be upfront with you on cost before anything is built — no surprises.
What if it breaks or stops working? +
We monitor your system and fix issues as part of the service. You're not on your own trying to figure out why something stopped working. If you notice something off, you contact us and we fix it — usually the same day. This isn't a product you buy and then manage yourself. We stay involved.
How fast will I see results? +
The instant response system is designed to work from day one — the moment a lead comes in, they hear back automatically. How quickly that translates into closed jobs depends on your business and your lead volume. The follow-up and quoting systems build their impact over time as leads move through the pipeline. We'll set realistic expectations on your strategy call based on what you're actually working with.
Will the automated messages sound weird or robotic to my customers? +
We write the messages to sound like you — direct, friendly, professional. Your customers won't know it's automated. We'll show you every message before it goes live so you can read through it and tell us if it doesn't sound right. We adjust until it does.
Do I have to sign a long-term contract? +
No. We're month to month. We don't lock you in because we don't think we should need to. If the system is working and saving you time and closing more jobs, you'll want to keep it. If for some reason it's not working for you, you can walk away. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you into it.
